Nashville Hot Chicken

Prep: 20 minutes

Cook: 16 minutes

Additional: 9 hours 24 mintues

Total: 10 hours

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of kosher salt

Marinade:

  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup of pickle brine
  • 2 tablespoons of Lousiana-style hot sauce
  • 1 large egg

Flour:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of fine table salt

Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of lard
  • 2 tablespoons of cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Toss chicken pieces with 1 tablespoon of kosher salt. Transfer chicken to a bowl;cover

    and refrigerate overnight.

  2. Step 2

    3. Whisk buttermilk, pickle bring, hot sauce, and egg together in a mixing bowl. Pour

    marinade over chicken and stir to ensure each piece is thoroughly coated. Cover and

    let chicken marinate in refrigerator for 2 to 4 hours.

  3. Step 3

    4. Mix flour and salt together in a shallow flat dish. Remove chicken from marinade and

    blot with paper towels. Reserve marinade. Toss chicken in flour until thoroughly

    coated. Return chicken to marinade, coating all sides;remove chicken from marinade

    allowing excess to drip back into the bowl. Coat the chicken once again in flour

    mixture. Place on a rack. Repeat with all chicken pieces. Allow to sit out about 15

    minutes to allow coating to dry out a bit.

  4. Step 4

    5. Place butter and lard in a large pot. Add cayenne pepper, brown sugar, paprika, garlic

    powder, kosher salt,and black pepper. Place over medium-high heat. Cook and stir

    until fats melt. Remove from heat;keep sauce warm.

  5. Step 5

    6. Fill cast iron skillet about 1/3 of the way with vegetable oil. Heat oil to 350 degrees Fahrenheit

    over medium-high heat. Carefully place chicken into hot oil, skin side down. Maintain

    an oil temperature of 325 degrees Fahrenheit, adjusting heat as needed. Fry until instant read

    thermometer readys 160 degrees Fahrenheit, 8 to 10 minutes per side.

  6. Step 6

    7. Transfer chicken to a rack to drain. Brush with the sauce on both sides

Lasagna

Prep: 20 minutes

Cook: 2 hours 45 minutes

Additional: 20 mintues

Total: 3 hours 25 mintues

Servings: 12

Yield: 1 10x15-inch pan

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 1/2 pounds of lean ground beef
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of fresh mushrooms, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried Italian herb seasoning (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 6 cups of prepared marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of water, plus more as needed
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 pounds of whole-milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) container of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 2/3 cups of freshly shredded Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch of cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of lasagna noodles
  • 1 (8 ounce) container of fresh mozzarella cheese, diced
  • 1/2 cup of freshly shredded Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Place sausage and ground beef into a large saucepan over medium heat;cook and stir

    until meat is browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes. Break the meat apart as it

    cooks. Stir in mushrooms, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper, Italian herb

    seasoning, and red pepper flakes. Turn heat to medium-high and cook until

    mushrooms have given off their juices and bottof of pan is almost dry.

  2. Step 2

    3. Pour prepared marinara sauce into meat mixture. Pour water into marinara sauce jar

    and shake to get all the sauce out of the jar;add to mixture. Reduce heat to low and

    simmer until meat is extremely tender, about 2 hours. Add a little more water if sauce

    becomes too thick. Skim excess fat from surface of the sauce and season with more

    salt and black pepper,if needed.Turn off heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius.)

  4. Step 4

    5. Beat eggs in a large bowl and stir in ricotta cheese, 8 ounced diced mozzarella cheese,

    and 2/3 cups of Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. Season cheese mixture with 1 teaspoon

    salt,1/4 teaspoon black pepper,and cayenne pepper;mix in parsley

  5. Step 5

    6. Bring a large pot of salter water to a boil. Cook lasagna in the boiling water, stirring

    occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and

    rinse noodles and set aside in a bowl of cold water

  6. Step 6

    7. Spread 1/4 of the sauce into the bottom of a 10x15-inch baking pan. Top with 1/3 the

    noodles and spread 1/2 the ricotta cheese mixture over noodles. Spread 1/4 of the meat

    sauce over the cheese, top with 1/3 of the noodles, and spread remaining 1/2 of the ricotta

    mixture over the noodles. Spread 1/4 of the meat sauce over the cheese filling. Tap the

    casserole dish lightly on a sturdy work surface to settle the layers. Arrange last 1/3 of the

    noodles over sauce and spread remaining 1/4 of the sauce over the noodles. Dot top of

    the casserole with 4 ounces of diced fresh mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with 1/2 cup

    of Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.

  7. Step 7

    8. Cover casserole with aluminum foil, being sure not to touch the top of the casserole

    with the foil. Place casserole onto a baking sheet to catch spills.

  8. Step 8

    9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove foil and cook until lasagna is

    golden brown and bubbling, 30 to 35 more minutes. Let set for 20 minutes before

    cutting into squares
